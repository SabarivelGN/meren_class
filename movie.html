Top 5 Ajith Kumar Movies

Celebrating the charisma and screen presence of Thala Ajith through five of his most iconic Tamil films.

Vaali
Released: 1999
  • Psychological Thriller
  • Ajith in dual roles
Cast: Ajith, Simran
Director: S. J. Surya
Producer: S. S. Chakravarthy
Summary: A suspenseful tale of twin brothers and obsession, marking Ajith's breakout performance in a complex dual role.
Dheena
Released: 2001
  • Action, Drama
  • Thala nickname origin
Cast: Ajith, Laila
Director: A. R. Murugadoss
Producer: Venu Ravichandran
Summary: A loyal younger brother caught between family and gang rivalry, this film earned Ajith the title “Thala” among fans.
Billa
Released: 2007
  • Stylish Action
  • Remake of Rajinikanth’s classic
Cast: Ajith, Nayanthara
Director: Vishnuvardhan
Producer: L. Suresh
Summary: A stylish gangster thriller where Ajith’s screen presence and sleek action sequences elevated him to superstar status.
Vedalam
Released: 2015
  • Action, Sentiment
  • Brother-sister bond
Cast: Ajith, Shruti Haasan
Director: Siva
Producer: A. M. Rathnam
Summary: A loving brother with a violent past turns avenger, blending emotional depth and mass action in true Thala style.
Viswasam
Released: 2019
  • Family Drama, Action
  • Rural mass entertainer
Cast: Ajith, Nayanthara
Director: Siva
Producer: Sathya Jyothi Films
Summary: A father returns to protect his daughter, with colorful rural vibes and emotional storytelling that resonated with fans.
